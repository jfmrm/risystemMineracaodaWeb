<h1>Computer Science > Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition</h1>
</div>
<h1 class="title mathjax"><span class="descriptor">Title:</span>
Linear Algebra and Duality of Neural Networks</h1>
<div class="authors"><span class="descriptor">Authors:</span>
<a href="/search/cs?searchtype=author&query=Georgiev%2C+G">Galin Georgiev</a></div>
<div class="dateline">(Submitted on 12 Sep 2018)</div>
<blockquote class="abstract mathjax">
<span class="descriptor">Abstract:</span> Natural for Neural networks bases, mappings, projections and metrics are
built. Graph-theoretical interpretation is offered. Non-Gaussianity naturally
emerges, even in relatively simple datasets. Training statistics and
hierarchies are discussed, from physics point of view. Relationship between
exact and numerical solutions is looked into. Duality between observables and
observations is established. Examples support all new concepts.
</blockquote>
